# Retail Sales & Profitability Analytics

## Business Problem

The CEO observed that overall sales continued to increase, but profitability was not growing at the same rate.

This project analyzes nearly 10,000 retail transactions to identify the key drivers behind declining profit margins and recommends actionable strategies to improve business performance.

## Key Insights

- High discounts increased revenue but reduced profitability.
- Several products generated strong sales while consistently losing money.
- A small group of customers contributed a significant share of total profits.
- Some states remained unprofitable despite healthy sales performance.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Reduce excessive discounting.
- Reassess loss-making products.
- Prioritize marketing in high-profit regions.
- Develop retention programs for high-value customers.
